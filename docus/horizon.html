Horizon Bootstrap System

Horizon is the automated deployment engine that transitions a host system into a standalone Astaraxia installation.

The Three Stages

Stage 1: Building the temporary toolchain. (Est: 2-4 hours)

Stage 2: Chroot preparation and base system build. (Est: 3-5 hours)

Stage 3: Final system configuration and cleanup. (Est: 6-10 hours)

Getting Started

Ensure your environment is ready by exporting your target partition:

export LFS=/mnt/astaraxia
sudo astral -h --init

Detailed build instructions are available at the Horizon Repository.
